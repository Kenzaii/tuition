Booking FAQs

Common questions about our booking process

What is your cancellation policy?

You can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled session without any penalty. For cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance, a 50% fee will apply. No-shows will be charged the full session fee.

How do online sessions work?

Online sessions are conducted via Zoom. After your booking is confirmed, you'll receive an email with the Zoom link and instructions. You'll need a computer or tablet with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection. Our tutors use interactive digital whiteboards and screen sharing to create an engaging learning experience.

Can I request a specific tutor?

Yes, if you have a preference for a specific tutor, please mention this in the "Special Requests" section when booking. We'll do our best to accommodate your request based on the tutor's availability. If you don't have a preference, we'll match you with the most suitable tutor for your subject and level.

What should I bring to the session?

For in-person sessions, please bring your textbooks, notes, and any relevant learning materials. It's also helpful to bring recent assessments or areas you're struggling with. For online sessions, please have these materials accessible on your device or physically beside you. A notebook and pen/pencil are always recommended regardless of the mode.
