Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling us directly at +65 1234 5678.

How do I schedule a free consultation?

You can schedule a free consultation by using our booking page, filling out the contact form, or calling us directly. We'll arrange a time that works for both parties to discuss your specific needs.

Can I visit your center without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that appropriate staff members are available to address your specific questions and needs. This helps us provide you with the most relevant information and dedicated attention.

Is there a waiting list for your tuition services?

Availability varies depending on the subject, level, and time of year. During peak periods (exam seasons), some of our popular tutors may have waiting lists. We recommend contacting us as early as possible to secure your preferred tutor and schedule.
